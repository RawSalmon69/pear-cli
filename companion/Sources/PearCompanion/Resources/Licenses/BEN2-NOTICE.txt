BEN2 background-removal model (High-quality mode)

When you turn on High-quality background removal, Pear downloads and runs,
on-device, a Core ML conversion of BEN2 Base by Prama LLC.

  BEN2 — https://github.com/PramaLLC/BEN2
  Model card — https://huggingface.co/PramaLLC/BEN2

BEN2 Base is released under the MIT License, which permits commercial use. Only
the open Base model is used here (Prama LLC's proprietary "full" model is not).
